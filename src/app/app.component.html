Welcome to BlackOut

BlackOut is an escape room in which you will need to put together clues in order to move into the next room. Click on objects in the room to find hints. If you "grab" an item, it will be added to your inventory for later use. When you think you've solved a clue, click the item in your inventory then click the object you think it matches with. When items match up, they will disappear, usually allowing access to a new clue! Use the directional arrows at the bottom of the page to see what is to the left, right, and front of you in each room!
